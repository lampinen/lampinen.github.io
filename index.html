I am interested in cognitive flexibililty and generalization, and how these abilities are enabled by factors like language, memory, and embodiment. I consider these issues from both the perspective of human cognition and artificial intelligence.

I am a Research Scientist at DeepMind. I completed my PhD in Cognitive Psychology at Stanford University. Prior to that, my background is in mathematics, physics, and machine learning.
